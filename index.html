4Li-Ion BB is NOT being installed on a non-standard rack.

2
✅ Installation Acceptance
checklist
▼
Verify each item below before proceeding. Comment is mandatory if NOK is selected.
#ItemOKNOKComment (mandatory if NOK)
1Every battery manufacturing date is NOT older than 6 months from installation date.
2Every battery has Tawal Logo, Serial Number, and Hotline number.
3Battery rack (if new) is free of scratches, rust, and other physical damage signs.
4Battery rack is fixed to floor with bolts using Normal & Spring washers.
5Battery rack is positioned on right side of shelter with min. 20 cm clearance from A/C grilles and min. 5 cm from DC cabinet.
6Each battery is fixed with 4 bolts + spring washers to the rack.
7No. of Li-Ion batteries installed on same battery rack is ≤ 10.
8Uniform 1U (~= 4.5 cm) clearance gap between batteries is maintained.
9No battery is installed on top of battery rack structure.
10Battery rack is grounded by min. 16 mm² cable connected directly to main grounding busbar.
11No. of Li-Ion batteries paralleled to same DC system is ≤ 16.
12All battery cable ends crimped to lugs with heat-shrink isolation.
13All DC cables from DC system to batteries are the same length and CSA.
14DC cable CSA: 25 mm² if distance ≤ 5 m; 35 mm² if distance > 5 m. Interconnection cables are same CSA.
15All Positive DC cables are Red.
16All Negative DC cables are Blue.
17All Grounding cables are Yellow/Green.
18Each battery is connected to a dedicated 125A DC-CB or fuse.
19If 2 batteries share a fuse: rating is 250A and CSA is 50 mm². If not possible, Tawal PM must be notified.
20All cables are properly routed over cable tray and fixed to rack with cable ties.
21Rectifier modules meet N+1 configuration (N = modules for max site load + min. 10A charge per battery).
22Battery type, count, and Ah capacity is correctly configured on DC system MCU.
23DC system MCU date/time is set to Riyadh time (GMT+3).
24Battery BMS date/time is set to Riyadh time (GMT+3).
25Anti-theft (gyroscope) protection is activated and tested.
26Battery lock is ONLY activated upon gyroscope 'Anti-theft' threshold breach.
27All pre-configuration values are validated on every battery (Ref. BMS Alarm & Protection Thresholds).
28Red LED is activated whenever battery is locked or protected.
29Overcharge protection ONLY blocks charging — discharge remains possible.
30Battery dry contact alarm cables are connected in series to DC system and configured as a major alarm.
31Battery external alarm cables are labeled inside DC system.
32Battery external alarms are simulated and tested.
33All battery indicator lights are GREEN with no alarms.
34After installation, batteries were connected to DC system ONE BY ONE.
35Battery software, cable, and 1st-level password handed to O&M MSP; MSP acknowledged on GCL.
36Stamped battery warranty certificate from supplier (S/N, brand, site ID, install date, mfg date, project name) handed to MSP.
37Stamped rack galvanization & warranty certificate (site IDs, dates, color code, thickness) handed to MSP.
38Old batteries, racks, cables, and waste materials are removed from site.
39Site is free of hazards and safety risks.
40Handover GCL is filled, dated, signed and stamped as per Tawal standard (battery serial numbers and details included).
